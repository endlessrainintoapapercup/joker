📘 Player's Guide

Joker's Gambit v5 — Complete Reference

Overview

Joker's Gambit is a poker-based deckbuilder. Each run lasts 10 rounds. In every round you play poker hands from your deck to accumulate enough points to hit the target score before running out of hands. Win a round and you earn gold and a new Joker; fail and the run ends.

Rounds 3, 6, and 9 feature a Shop where you can spend your gold on Jokers, Consumables, and Run Modifiers. Your deck is locked for the entire run — choose it carefully at the start.

1 · How Scoring Works

Every hand you play produces a score using this formula:

Score = (Base Chips + Card Values + Bonus Chips) × (Base Mult + Mult Bonus)

Base Chips & Base Mult come from the poker hand type:

HandChipsMult
High Card5×1
Pair10×2
Two Pair20×2
Three of a Kind30×3
Straight30×4
Flush35×4
Full House40×4
Four of a Kind60×7
Straight Flush100×8
Royal Flush100×8

Card Values only count from scoring cards (the cards forming the hand pattern).
Number cards = face value  |  J/Q/K = 10  |  Ace = 11 (20 in Commoner's Deck)

See some worked examples:

Full House (A♠ A♥ K♣ K♦ Q♠) + Zany Joker:
Base: 40 chips × 4 mult  |  Card values: 11+11+10+10+10 = 52  |  Zany Joker: +12 mult
Score = (40 + 52) × (4 + 12) = 92 × 16 = 1,472 pts

The score bar shows progress toward the round target. Surplus points carry over at 5% to the next round. Each hand you play draws back up to your hand size from the deck automatically.

2 · How Joker Effects Differ

Jokers fire after every hand and fall into four distinct effect types. Understanding the difference is crucial for building a strong run.

➕ Adds Chips

Increases the left side of the formula. Good early on but scales poorly — large mult values dilute chip gains.
Examples: Midas Touch, Royal Court, Banner.

➕ Base Mult (additive)

Added to Base Mult before the final multiplication. Very powerful at moderate mult levels.
Examples: Road Runner, Zany Joker, Even Steven.

➕ Additive Mult Bonus

Adds directly to the mult total. Some jokers add both chips and mult. They resolve before multiplicative jokers.
Examples: Scholar, Scary Face, Fibonacci.

✖ Multiplicative Mult

Multiply the entire current mult — not just add to it. Fire after all additive jokers. Rarest and most powerful.
Examples: Baron, Loyalty Card, Bloodstone, The Duo.

💡 Key insight: A joker that adds +10 mult when your total mult is 8 is a 225% score increase. The same joker when your mult is 50 is only a 25% boost. Always stack additive mult first, then seek multiplicative jokers.

8 · The Gambit

Once per round (first hand only) you can declare The Gambit. You must score 1.5× the round target in that single hand. Success instantly clears the round and awards +3g. Failure deducts 2g (never below 0) — the round continues normally. Unavailable on the final round (Round 10).

💡 Best used on the first hand of a round when you have strong mult jokers lined up. The gambit resets every round — use it freely!

9 · Starter Strategies

♥ Red Flush Machine

Deck: Mirrored  |  Jokers: Suited Up · Bloodstone · Droll Joker
Double hearts and diamonds make flushes trivially easy. Droll Joker adds +10 mult on each flush; Suited Up boosts base mult by 50%; Bloodstone multiplies per ♥. Convert key cards to ♥ with The Sun consumable to max procs.

📈 Pair Snowball

Deck: Standard or Short  |  Jokers: The Duo · Wee Joker · Supernova
Play Pairs every hand. The Duo ×2 mult on pure Pairs; Wee Joker permanently accumulates chips and mult; Supernova rewards repetition. By round 6 each Pair hand is dramatically more powerful.

👑 King's Face Avalanche

Deck: King's Court  |  Jokers: Baron · Royal Court · Scary Face
60 cards with 8 Kings. Hold Kings in hand (never play them) for Baron's ×1.5 per King. Use High Card or Pair plays to retain Kings as long as possible.

🔢 Number Cruncher

Deck: Commoner's  |  Jokers: Fibonacci · Scholar · The Alchemist
No face cards, but Aces = 20 chips and +2 mult per hand. Fibonacci fires on A/2/3/5/8 for +8 mult each. The Alchemist converts your chip surplus into even more mult.

💡 Universal tips: Unspent gold is lost when you leave the shop — spend everything. Jokers are the strongest investment. Mult jokers scale better than chip jokers in later rounds. Funnel all 3 jokers toward the same hand type for maximum trigger frequency.
